MY TOP 10 FAVORITE SHOWS
1.WESTWORLD


A 2016 adaptation on the 1973 film of the same name, this series explores societal reliance on technology, human morals, and ideas about the bicameral mind.

The cinematography, plot, character design and development are among the many reasons why it is my top 1 show. The fourth season is set to release this year.

2.GAME OF THRONES


Game of Thrones is a 2011 adaptation roughly based on Georgr R.R. Martin's "A Song of Ice and Fire". It follows the great houses of the seven kingdoms as they vye for power and the iron throne.
Although the ending was rushed, it is still achieved many a great feat for a television series.

3.EUPHORIA


Euphoria follows the story of teenager, Rue Bennett and her battle against drug addiction. It dives deep into the behaviour of troubled teenagers and how they cope with experiences of love, friendship, abuse, trauma, and identity.
We haven't exactly been served with a happy ending for some characters, but it shows us the harsh reality of being a misguided individual.

4.THE WITCHER


The Witcher is a Netflix fantasy drama web television series creted by Lauren Schmidt Hissrich. It is based on the novel series of the same name by Polish author Andrzej Sapkowski. Geralt of Rivia, a lone monster hunter, strives to find his place in a world where people are frequently more cruel than monsters and beasts.

5.LOKI


The second series from the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Loki revolves around the mischievous villain escaping the clutches of The Avengers and getting caught by the Time Variance Authority. This sends him on a mission to catch the different antagonist who has been troubling the timelines.

6.THE WALKING DEAD


In a world where the word "zombie" or the concept of the undead does not exist, Walking dead tells the story of Rick Grimes and a ragtag group of survivors who travel in search of a safe and secure home. It is a pretty long series as it follows their journey through months and years into the apocalypse.

7.AMERICAN GODS


American Gods is an adaptation of Neil Gaiman'g book of the same name. It follows Shadow Moon, a man serving three years in prison who is given an early release. He is hired as Mr. Wednesday's bodyguard and finds himself in a hidden world where a battle is brewing between the Old Gods and the New Gods.

8.STRANGER THINGS


A Netflix Original series released in 2016.

After the mysterious and sudden vanishing of a young boy, the people of a small town begin to uncover secrets of a government lab, portals to another world and sinister monsters.

9.THE MAGICIANS


A SyFy (tv channel) exclusive series.

Quentin Coldwater enrolls at Brakebills University for Magical Pedagogy to be trained as a magician, where he discovers that the magical world from his favorite childhood book is real and poses a danger to humanity.

10.AMERICAN HORROR STORY


An FX (tv channel) exclusive anthology series created by Ryan Murphy.
Each season is a different horror story, but with the same actors from previous seasons playing new roles. The stories are engaging and present historical evidence although shallow which makes it even more horryfiing.
