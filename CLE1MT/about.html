ABOUT US

Children require and desire caring and secure interactions with their families. As a result, we are their family until they are formally adopted. We take a long-term approach to caring for these children, combining physical, social, economic, and spiritual care to ensure that they receive all of the love and guidance they require until they are adopted into their forever homes. That is our long-term goal.

WE NEED YOUR HELP!
We would not be able to help all of these children by ourselves, we need as much help as we can get.

• ADOPTION
There are 2,000,000 orphans and currently over 26,000,000 households in the Philippines, about 30% could afford to care for another child. Through adoption we can give these children a safe, stable, and permanent home they can finally settle in.
